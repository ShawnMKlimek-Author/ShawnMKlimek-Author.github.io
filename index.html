About the Author

Click for another view

Shawn M. Klimek is a speculative fiction author and poet whose work spans science fiction, fantasy, horror, and humorous verse. With dozens of published short stories and poems appearing in anthologies and magazines across multiple continents, he writes with equal comfort in the darkly macabre and the delightfully absurd.

He is currently at work on two short story collections — a science fiction anthology and a fantasy anthology — both forthcoming under Jot in the Dark Publishing.

His work has appeared in publications by Black Hare Press, Clarendon House, Zombie Pirate Publishing, Spillwords Press, NewMyths, and many others. He has also won recognition in the Winning Writers Wergle Flomp Humorous Poetry Contest.

Published Books

Hungry Thing

Written by Shawn M. Klimek  ·  Illustrated by Norbert Somosi

A dark fantasy story told in five poems — Hungry Thing follows the fate of a young girl named Gladys in a tale that is by turns lyrical, eerie, and unforgettable. Praised by reviewers as "something Lovecraft might do if Lovecraft wrote poetry," this debut collection is a collaboration between word and image unlike any other.

"It's extremely rare to read a book of any kind in which the sentences, the phrases, sing — but Hungry Thing does just that."
