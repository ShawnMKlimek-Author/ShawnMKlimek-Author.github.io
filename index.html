About the Author

Shawn M. Klimek

Click to see author photo

Shawn M. Klimek is a speculative fiction author and poet whose work spans science fiction, fantasy, horror, and humorous verse. With dozens of published short stories and poems appearing in anthologies and magazines across multiple continents, he writes with equal comfort in the darkly macabre and the delightfully absurd.

His debut collection, Hungry Thing, is available via Lulu. He is currently at work on two short story collections: a science fiction anthology and a fantasy anthology, both forthcoming under Jot in the Dark Publishing.

Klimek's work has appeared in publications by Black Hare Press, Clarendon House, Zombie Pirate Publishing, Spillwords Press, NewMyths, and many others. He has also won recognition in the Winning Writers Wergle Flomp Humorous Poetry Contest.
